Print Julep

Designs Fresh As Mint

A Central Florida based creative company specializing in giving life to ideas through inspired design.

Print Julep is a graphic arts and lifestyle brand based in Oviedo, Florida founded and owned by Myla Miranda. The company un-officially launched via facebook.com in May 2018. What began as a small business based out of Myla's home office is growing quickly.


Current Works

Print Julep was recently commissioned to paint the frontman and lead singer for the iconic band Queen, Freddy Mercury. This piece will completed in acrylic paint on bristol paper. Once completed it will be dontaed to a silent auction to benefit the Starving Artist's Foundation.
